Our Mission

At Helping Hands, we believe every child deserves the right to education, love, and opportunity — no matter their background or ability. Our mission is to support poor and differently-abled children by providing access to inclusive education, nutritious meals, mental health care, and life skills training.

We work hand-in-hand with communities and volunteers to nurture every child’s full potential. Join us in building a more compassionate and inclusive world.

Our Impact

📚 3,000+ children enrolled in schools
🦽 500+ children received mobility aids
🍎 Daily meals to 1,200+ children
🎨 Creative workshops and inclusive play
